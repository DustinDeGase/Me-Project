The Good Ole Days

There are times in life that feel so lost. And there are times in life that feel so found. There are good and bad. There is up. There is down. There is air, literally all around. This to you, that to me, hold on one sec, I have to pee.
An old man once walked down the road. He stopped by a carrot cart and asked the vender his price. "Three shillings" replied the carrot dealer. "Three shillings!" yelled the old man. "That's bullshit!"
Approaching a hip kid on a bike i thought i would impress him with a sweet moving dismount from my own bike. Instead of cleanily hopping off, my dated shorts caught the seat ripping said shorts from my body causing me to crash into the shop.
One time i walked into the bathroom and found Alex hovering above the toilet, one foot on each side of the rim in a sort of lineman hunch. And in between his legs hung a turd. Welcome to parenthood.
